Price alerts by email

Set a low and high price for each ticker. A scheduled job checks prices and emails you when either threshold is hit.

How it will work

  1. Watchlist lives in watchlist.json in this repo.
  2. GitHub Actions runs hourly: pulls prices from Yahoo Finance.
  3. When a ticker crosses your low or high price, an email goes out via Gmail SMTP.
  4. State is tracked so you only get one email per crossing, not repeats.
